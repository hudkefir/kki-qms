## WHAT THE PLANNER DOES (study planner-source-reference.html)
Core features to port:
1. **Production scheduling** — weekly view with date ranges, bin counts per day
2. **Purchase order management** — CRUD for POs with SKUs, quantities, ship dates, FIFO allocation
3. **Batch management** — batch records with production dates, bin counts, SKU, inventory tracking
4. **Fermentation pipeline** — GRP batches tracking ferment→strain→ready stages with dates
5. **Small fridge tracking** — separate GRP fridge inventory
6. **FIFO allocation engine** — oldest-batch-first allocation against POs
7. **Inventory by SKU** — real-time inventory view
8. **Pour batch records** — tracking pours from GRP to finished product
9. **Physical inventory counts & verification audits** — count vs system reconciliation
10. **Charts** — demand vs production, bins scheduled vs needed (Chart.js)
11. **Dark mode, print views, data export**
